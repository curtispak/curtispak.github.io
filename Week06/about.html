My name is Curtis Pak and I am mechanical keyboards enthusaist currently residing in NYC.

I was first introduced into the hobby when I bought my first "gaming" mechanical keyboard. For some odd reason, I fell in love with the way it sounded and felt. Due to that, I set out on a journey to find the "best" keyboard there is. After obtaining and building various keyboards, I want to help others obtain their own custom mechanical keyboard.
